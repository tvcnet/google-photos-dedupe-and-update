Joining point notes

Day 1 begins in Athens with a 6:00 PM welcome meeting and dinner after arrival at the Athenaeum joining point.

  • It is important to attend the welcome meeting because insurance details and next-of-kin information are collected then.
  • If plans change or arrival runs late, notify the travel agent or hotel reception and check the lobby for meeting information.
  • Taxi from Athens Airport to central Athens typically takes 30 to 45 minutes and costs about EUR 50 depending on time of day.
  • Metro line 3 to Syntagma, then line 2 toward Elliniko to Acropolis station, leaves the hotel roughly 450 meters away.

Typical Conditions

Late April to early May weather

Expect comfortable spring touring weather across Athens, the mainland route, and Santorini, with sunny days, cooler evenings, and slightly breezier island sunsets.

Athens

Sunny spring city and mainland touring days with dry air and cool evenings.

65 to 75°F 18 to 24°C Mostly sunny

Expect bright afternoons and easy layering for Athens, Nafplion, Delphi, Olympia, and the central mainland route, with cooler mornings inland.

Santorini

Sun-filled island weather with sea breeze building near sunset.

64 to 73°F 18 to 23°C Sunset breeze

Plan for warm daytime sightseeing and a light layer for the May 3 Viator tour as the coastal breeze picks up in the late afternoon and evening.
